# 3-Minute Demo Script

## 0:00-0:20 Problem
Hospitality emergencies move fast. Guest reports, staff updates and responder communication are often fragmented across phone calls, WhatsApp groups and front-desk logs.

## 0:20-0:45 Solution
CrisisBridge AI gives a hotel a QR-first emergency intake page and a live staff command dashboard. Gemini converts messy reports into structured severity, incident type, response actions and responder briefings.

## 0:45-1:30 Guest Report
Open the guest form. Submit a smoke/fire report for Floor 3 with a short description. Show that the guest does not need an app install.

## 1:30-2:15 Command Dashboard
Switch to the dashboard. Show the new incident at the top, severity, zone highlight, timeline, AI summary, next actions and team assignment.

## 2:15-2:40 Coordination
Update status from New to Responding, assign Security or Medical, and show the responder briefing.

## 2:40-3:00 Impact
Close with the impact: faster triage, less confusion, better coordination, and a deployable Google Cloud architecture using Gemini.
